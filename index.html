About

ISTE Manipal presents Crown Jewel our very own Squid Games themed online coding contest where you can compete along side your team mates against the best coders and put your skills to test. The event contains 3 rounds spread over 2 days. The coders get to play a fair game under the identical conditions and only one will emerge as the winner. Grab this chance to win amazing cash prizes.

Rules

Round 1 : Deadlocked

  • Durations: 2hrs
  • Groups of 3-4 are allowed. (single players will be allotted the group randomly)
  • After participating each player needs to submit his/her Hackerrank ID, and a team leader ID needs to be marked. The team leaders ID will only be used to submit the answer to the given question.
  • Special care needs to be taken if a team leader decides to drop out.
  • After this round the list of selected teams for round 2 will be displayed on the leaderboard on the website itself.
  • **Only responses submitted by the team leader will be considered**
