About

ISTE Manipal presents to you - Crown Jewel , our very own Squid Games themed online coding contest where you can compete against the best coders and put your skills to the ultimate test. The event consists of three rounds spread over the course of two days. All participants get to play a fair game under identical conditions, but only one will emerge as the winner. Grab this chance to win fantastic cash prizes!
